About Us

Chasing Tails is a not for profit organization that aims to rescue, rehabilitate and rehome abandoned cats and dogs. With the mission to make the world a better place for all abandoned pets, the Center offers veterinary care, adoption services and care advice for cats and dogs. Our pets are adorable and have their own unique personalities, which are sure to delight anyone looking to bring a pet into their lives. Explore our site to learn more about the pets in our shelter or sign up for our mailing list to attend one of our Meet the Pets events!
